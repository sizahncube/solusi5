PHILOSOPHY The Faculty of Education and Humanities is based on the recognition that teaching and learning forms the core foundation in the development of holistic, responsible, productive graduates who can effectively contribute to the social, economic, political and cultural development of humanity. The Faculty of Education and Humanities therefore, promotes the acquisition of knowledge, skills and competencies culminating in the growth of students who are knowledgeable, reflective, creative, innovative and competent.

MISSION STATEMENT The primary goal of the Faculty of Education and Humanities is to develop competent, knowledgeable practitioners with the capacity to teach, transmit skills, implement the curriculum, and carry out educational and social research to solve problems in different life situations. The faculty assumes the responsibility of developing the students’ great potential and thus building resonance with the Creator
