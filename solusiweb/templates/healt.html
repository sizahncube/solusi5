Faculty of Health Professions

PHILOSOPHY The philosophy of the Faculty of Health Professions is based on Jesus’ mandate. Jesus was and is the greatest healer of the mind, soul, and body. Thus, as His followers we are commissioned to continue His healing ministry till He returns. The healing ministry is continued through education, service, and knowledge discovery. Students in this Faculty will be educated and trained to prevent, identify, and treat health related human problems and evaluate health risks in the environment. Students will also be proficient in providing hope, spiritual comfort, and teaching of biblical principles of healthy living.

MISSION STATEMENT The mission of the Faculty of Health Professions is in line with the University’s mission and the tenets of the Seventh-Day Adventist Church. The mission of the faculty is to prepare health professionals whose values are Christ-centered, and are competent in health-care delivery, research, and teaching of health principles.
